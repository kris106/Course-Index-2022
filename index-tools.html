Designing Without Design Tools



Design a 24” × 36” digital poster about either 1) your life under COVID 19 or 2) how you see the future of design.

Without using software explicitly intended for design.



Over the course of this project I started with the intention to design a visual commenting on my life under Covid-19. I began ideating designs, which resulted in thinking about the future of design more and more now that everything is online during Covid-19.

I think the future of design holds a spectrum of style ranging from the precision of Swiss design to the recent trend of generative art seen in large animated displays and NFTs. The idea that I was trying to convey is the transformation of modern design, which is heavily influenced by Swiss design moving into experimental styles such as anti-design and generative art. I show this transition by creating a Swiss design styled poster that connects through QR code to a generative art/anti-design poster. Accessible through the lens of a smartphone, this is a commentary on the world of art transitioning online.

Tools Used

  • Google Slides - Picture assembly and shapes


  • VQGAN + CLIP in Google Collab – Generative art maker


  • Nightcafe Creator – Generative art maker


  • Img.Upscaler – Improve generative art quality


  • Chaotica – Unused in final but used in experimenting


  • PhotoMania – Image filters
