Code Sketches



I developed a daily practice of "code sketches". Rather than constructing a single project, my assigned goal was to allot daily time to exploring generative art (30 minutes to 1 hour). Everyone in the class got to choose a letter spelling out a phrase I picked the letter Y. I created 21 interactive code sketches of the letter Y in p5 (Javascript) over the course of 3 weeks.

Why “Sketch” Daily - Project Brief

This module does not ask you to develop a deep conceptual component to your work. Instead, the goal is to experiment with tools daily. Sketches provide the opportunity to build capacity with your tools by building momentum. You’re not expected to spend hours a day on your task, but instead just allot 30 minutes to create a new sketch built off what you’ve previously done. Regular engagement with p5 will ensure a deeper understanding of its capacity and how you can deploy it. The hardest part of a daily practice is motivation to continue. I will regularly suggest you post your process on discord for your peers to be inspired and vice versa.
